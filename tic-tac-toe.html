About the Game

Tic-Tac-Toe, popularly knowns as X and O is a game...played by Two individuals.
The game can be played on Paper, Boards or on a Device just like this one.
The game is played on a 3 by 3 squared grid(s).

How to Play

The two players will decide on who will be Player O or Player X;
The first player to up to 3 marks in a row i.e up, down, across or diagonally is the winner.
If all boxes are filled and no player wins; The game will end in a tie(draw)!
